My favourite bands among them being КИНО (Kino) and Фристайл (Freestyle).

The main thing that draws me to programming is being able to solve problems. I really enjoy finding my own way of solving a problem and trying things out. I've been programming since I was about 10, first starting with very rudementary html. Later I learned python and played around with Raspberry Pi SBCs. My fascination of programming and computers stayed through my highschool time, where I've probably spent more time programming during than paying attention. As of now, I am studying for certificates (CPE and CPA) in C++.
